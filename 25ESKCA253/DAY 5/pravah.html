About Pravah

PRAVAH 2026 is the Annual Techno Cultural Sports Fest of SKIT Jaipur. Inspired by the theme "Nine Emotions | One Festival", the fest celebrates innovation, creativity, sportsmanship, music, dance, technology, literature and cultural diversity. Students from different colleges participate in exciting competitions, workshops and performances.
